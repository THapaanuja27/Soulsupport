Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are mental health quizzes?

Mental health quizzes are tools designed to help individuals assess their emotional and psychological well-being. They can identify areas that may need attention or professional support.

2. Are these quizzes accurate?

While these quizzes can provide insights, they are not diagnostic tools. For an accurate diagnosis or treatment plan, consult a mental health professional.

3. How should I use the results of the quizzes?

Use the results as a starting point for discussions with a healthcare provider. They can help interpret the results and suggest appropriate next steps.

4. Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, any information collected through the quizzes is kept confidential. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

5. Can I retake the quizzes?

Yes, you can retake the quizzes as often as you like to track changes in your mental health over time.
